Research Interests

  • Theoretical and empirical: prescriptive analytics, modeling, integer programming, constraint programming, binary and multi-valued decision diagrams, meta-heuristics, large neighborhood search, integration of optimization techniques.
  • Application areas: scheduling (sports, healthcare, workforce, machines), product line simplification, political districting, information layout, operations and logistics in general.
